Find the sum of digits of $a+6 b+21 c+36 d+32 e+720 f$:

$$
F_{100}-1=\sum_{i \in S} F_i ?
$$

Proving the identity is not very difficult. It can be shown by induction. If the result is true for $n$ then $\sum_{k=1}^{2n+2} F_k = \sum_{k=1}^{2n} F_k + F_{2n+1} + F_{2n+2} = F_{2n+2} + F_{2n+1} - 1 + F_{2n+2} = F_{2n+3} + F_{2n+2} - 1 = F_{2n+4} - 1$ completing the induction.
